# Lime Vinaigrette
_Yield: Makes about 3/4 cup_

## Ingredients
* ½ cup fresh lime juice 1 garlic clove, pressed 
* ¼ cup honey 
* ½ teaspoon salt
* ¼ teaspoon pepper
* ⅓ cup olive oil 

## Directions
Whisk together first 5 ingredients. Gradually whisk in 1/3 cup olive oil until blended.
